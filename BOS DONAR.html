"Saving Lives Through Donation."

Blood donation websites facilitate the donation of blood by connecting potential blood donors with blood banks and hospitals that need blood. Sperm donation websites allow sperm donors to connect with people or couples who want to have children but cannot do so naturally, due to infertility or other reasons. Organ donation websites aim to increase the number of people who are willing to donate organs after their death, by providing information on organ donation, and allowing individuals to register as organ donors. These websites also connect organ donors with those in need of organ transplants.
